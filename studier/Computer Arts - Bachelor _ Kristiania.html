Hva lærer du?

  • Vil du lage innhold til dataspill, film, TV eller nettsider? Computer Arts er et stort fagfelt med mange muligheter for dem som kan lage minneverdig innhold til ulike typer medier.
  • Denne utdanningen er unik i Norge, og gir deg et solid, faglig fundament – både teknisk og kunstnerisk.
  • Undervisningen foregår på engelsk, slik at du kan jobbe over hele verden.
  • Etter første semester velger du mellom én av fire spesialiseringer:
    • 3D-grafikk
    • Animasjon
    • Visual Effects (VFX)
    • Spilldesign
Mer om undervisningen

Mer om undervisningen

Med dette studieprogrammet vil du få en omfattende forståelse av digital innholdsproduksjon innen 3D-grafikk, animasjon, visuelle effekter eller spilldesign. Du vil utvikle både kreative og tekniske ferdigheter, mestre bransjestandard programvare og produksjonsteknikker samtidig som du lærer å skape engasjerende visuelle fortellinger.

Programmet legger vekt på praktisk erfaring, tverrfaglig samarbeid og virkelige anvendelser, noe som sikrer at du er godt rustet til å designe, animere og produsere arbeid av profesjonell kvalitet. I tillegg vil du bygge en sterk portefølje og utvikle essensielle ferdigheter innen teamarbeid, problemløsning og prosjektledelse, som alle er kritiske for suksess i den hurtiggående digitale kunstindustrien.

Åpne emneoverlegg

Introduction to Computer Arts

The course is an introduction to the Bachelor in Computer Arts programme and gives the student a basic overview of the subject with a focus on the specialisations in the study programme. Emphasis is placed on the student getting an overview of the four areas of study in the programme so that they can make an informed choice after the first semester. The students will work on computer arts related projects to gain insights into the specialisations. They will therefore gain a basic understanding of concepts, methods and tools within all fields of study.

Furthermore, the student will get an introduction to concepts and topics that cut across all fields of study. The student will also get an overview of the industries in which they will typically be able to work after completing their degree.

The subject also shows how the student goes about making a showreel which is the basis for a job application in the industry, and the student also makes his/hers first version of this.

7,5 stp.
Åpne emneoverlegg

IMPACT - Introduksjon til tverrfaglig samarbeid

Dette emnet gir en introduksjon til tverrfaglig samarbeid innen fagfeltene i film og medier, der utviklingsmetoder, selvstendig refleksjon og ansvar for egen læring står i sentrum. Emnet skal utforske den rollen tverrfaglig samarbeid kan ha i et samfunnsperspektiv.

Emnet har en undersøkende tilnærming til fagfeltene foto, klipp, lyddesign, manus, regi, produksjon, visual effects, spilldesign, 3D-grafikk og animasjon. Emnet tilbyr en introduksjon til vitenskapelige og kunstneriske metoder. Det skal også diskuteres etiske hensyn og perspektiver, med særlig vekt på inkludering, mangfold og bærekraft.

Det legges vekt på de felles faglige grunnelementer på tvers av fagene: idé- og konseptutvikling, planlegging og samarbeid i skapende prosesser. Studentene utvikler her begynnende forståelse for fagfeltenes roller.

Studentene skal jobbe sammen i tverrfaglige grupper, og vil få tilbakemelding på felles oppgaver løst i disse. Studentene skal introduseres for strukturert hverandrevurdering i kunstnerisk utvikling.

Studieretning
7,5 stp.
Åpne emneoverlegg

3D-graphics 1

Emnet 3D graphics 1 er et innledende emne i å jobbe med 3D-grafikk og legger grunnlaget for resten av studiet. Å jobbe med 3D-grafikk vil si å skape virtuelle objekter og scener for stillbilder, animasjon og spill/sanntidsapplkasjoner. Emnet gir studenten grunnleggende ferdigheter i modellering, materialer og teksturer, lyssetting av 3D-scener, og grunnleggende prinsipper rundt komposisjon og kamerateknikker for å skape og sette opp scener med 3D-grafikk.

Studieretning
7,5 stp.
Åpne emneoverlegg

3D-graphics 2

Emnet 3D graphics 2 er et videregående emne innen 3D-modellering og presentasjon av 3D-scener. Arbeidet innen dette emnet gir studenten dypere kunnskap og ferdigheter innen polygon-modellering, digital skuptering, teksturering og material-innstillinger for karakterer og scener. Emnet fokuserer også på hvordan studenten med lys, materialer og 'compositing' kan oppnå gode visuelle uttrykk i 3D-scener.

Studieretning
15 stp.
Åpne emneoverlegg

Characters and Environments

Med forbehold om endringer

The purpose of the course is to give students the knowledge and skills to be able to carry out advanced character and environment design with 3D graphics. The focus of the course is to create a thematic overall style with an emphasis on history and personality in an advanced scene with character and surroundings.

  • COA200Computer Arts Production 1

    The purpose of the course is to give the student a holistic experience with a larger computer arts production involving their specialization.

    The student will plan and carry out a comprehensive production in a group, and will receive training in both interdisciplinary teamwork and the use of modern techniques, methods and tools used for the organization and production of a large scale project. The student will be able to understand the different phases of production and how each group member contributes, as well as the different team roles.

  • WFM200Media Art Innovation Lab

    Media Art Innovation Lab (MAIL) er et tverrfaglig fellesemne ved Westerdals Institutt for Film og Medier (WIFM). 

    Dette emnet fungerer som et laboratorium for innovasjon, fremmer nye tenkemåter og samarbeidsmetoder, og gir studentene muligheten til å utforske nyskapende, kreative og eksperimentelle tilnærminger innenfor medie- og kunstfeltet. 

    En sentral del av emnet er tverrfaglig samarbeid. Studenter fra flere studieprogram jobber sammen i grupper for å planlegge, utvikle og gjennomføre prosjektarbeid. Dette bidrar til å utvikle studentenes samarbeidskompetanse, og gir dem muligheten til å respondere på og kommentere samfunnsspørsmål og den tiden vi lever i.   

    Studentene vil også bli introdusert for verdiperspektiver som bærekraftige vurderinger og prinsipper for grønn produksjon, samt prinsipper for mangfold og integrering. 

    I dette emnet er det studentene som driver innovasjon og kreative prosesser, og utvikler kunnskap og ny innsikt i en undervisningskontekst der de er aktive deltakere i læringsprosessen. 

  • 3DG114Realtime 3D Graphics

    The subject is about how to create good and aesthetic design and interactions in prototypes made with real-time 3D graphics. The students become familiar with the possibilities of the development environment and effective use of this. Students learn to use advanced materials and lighting in interactive scenes.

  • 3DG215Architectural Visualization Theory and Practice

    In this course students will learn how to visualize architecture or constructions in the different phases of a project. They are introduced to tools that are used for architectural visualization, and techniques applied for this.

  • ANI200Acting for Animators

    The course explores how acting techniques can be used in animation. To create characters with emotions and empathy, students are introduced to various aspects of acting techniques, including facial expressions for scenes with dialogue and improvisation. Through practical exercises, students will develop a deeper understanding of how to create scenes with characters that think, breathe, feel, and act.

    Students will also learn how to film themselves for reference and how to effectively use this material by analyzing and applying these insights to their animations.

  • ANI203Animation 3

    Animation 3 builds on the principles from Animation 1 and Animation 2. This course takes you deeper into the fascinating world of storytelling, acting techniques, and dramaturgy to create engaging scenes.

    During Animation 3, you will not only learn advanced animation techniques, such as facial animation, but also how to bring characters to life and imbue them with empathy throughout their journey. From miming to lively scenes with dialogue, we explore how to convey conflict, emotions, and personality through animation.

    We focus on detailing and polishing in the final phase of the animation process. Here, you will fine-tune asymmetry in posing, both in the body and face, as well as variations in curves, spacing, and all the small details that bring the character to life.

    Students will gain extensive experience in giving and receiving constructive feedback on their work.

  • SPD116Prototyping

    This course covers the fundamentals of system, UX, and UI design. Students learn to create, implement, and evaluate scalable game systems and intuitive, player-focused interfaces through prototypes. Emphasizing the connection between systems and UX/UI, the course teaches how to build gameplay-enhancing, usable designs. Projects include requirements analysis, human-centered design principles, flowcharts, wireframes, and progress from early prototypes to refined designs and user testing.

  • SPD209Narrative and Level Design

    In this course, the student will get a basic introduction to narrative structures linked to level design and visual storytelling techniques in games. The student will explore how video games tell stories, and how well-known principles from game and level design can enhance the narrative through mechanics, theme and mood. Through the course, the student will be introduced to narrative and level design, and gain practical experience with the design, development and iteration of virtual areas.

  • VFX221Cinematography

    The course gives students an introduction to photographic technique with a focus on composition and exposure/lighting. Students will learn to see subjects through the camera lens. Students also learn film as visual storytelling and how to use image composition, lighting and camera movement to tell a story. By filming their own VFX sequences, the students will increase their understanding of film and VFX techniques.

  • VFX2223D for VFX

    In this course the students will get an introduction to 3D graphics used as a tool towards work with visual effects (VFX). The students will gain basic skills in lighting and rendering of digital 3D objects with a view to integration in a VFX work process.

  • VAL999-7ENGElective course

    See elective courses.

  • COA300Computer Arts Production 2

    This course will provide students with relevant experience in executing a major practical project within their field of study, with high standards for quality and execution. The production could be a large-scale game, film/animation, VFX, or visualization project.

  • COA360Bachelor Production

    The student will gain professional experience by completing a project in a company, establishing their own business, or participating in a self-defined comprehensive research project. In a practical research project, the student will demonstrate broad knowledge of central topics and research methodology, and show skills in teamwork, project management methods, and relevant tools. The project should have a professional character and be of high quality.

    The course also includes a practical introduction to prepare the student for the industry. The student will gain knowledge about industry-specific law, the industry’s institutions and working methods, as well as tools such as application writing and self-presentation.

  • 3DG217Realtime 3D Graphics 2
  • ANI304Animation 4

    The course is an advanced animation course focusing on dramaturgy and behaviour for humans, animals, and creatures. The student will have the opportunity to develop their own expression and style. High standards are set for quality and execution in the last animation polishing phase.

  • SPD324System Design 2

    This course aims to provide students with a deeper understanding of system design through the lens of game balancing. Students will be introduced to advanced system design, which will be explored in detail. The course will cover various game mechanics, focusing on how these can be utilized, combined, and balanced to create a meaningful experience for the player.

  • VFX220Compositing 3

    Compositing 3 builds on the Compositing 2 course. The focus is on more advanced projects and typical advanced compositing tasks the students will encounter in the industry as well as completion of shots. Students will get to know some of the latest techniques and be able to make the final touches needed to deliver professional shots.

Hva kan du bli?

Nyutdannede fra Kristianias Bachelor i Computer Arts er rustet for ulike karrieremuligheter innen animasjon, spilldesign, 3D-grafikk og visuelle effekter, med roller innen film, TV, spill, reklame og mer. Tidligere Kristiania-studenter jobber i dag i selskaper som:
  • Funcom
  • Hyper Games
  • Attensi
  • Storm Studios
  • Plyo
  • Industrial Light & Magic
  • Gimpville
Flere karrieremuligheter

Flere karrieremuligheter

Du kan for eksempel jobbe som:

  • VFX-artist
  • Spilldesiner
  • Mediedesigner
  • VR-designer/utvikler

Tidligere studenter er svært ettertraktet i Computer Arts-industrien på grunn av den praktiske læringsprosessen de får ved Kristiania. I tillegg er noen av fagene utviklet av og undervist av ledende kunstnere innen sine felt. Dette skaper imponerende jobbmuligheter for våre alumner.

Visste du for eksempel at flere Hollywood-blockbusterfilmer og TV-serier har hatt sine visuelle effekter laget i Norge? Scener fra "The Last of Us", "Black Panther - Wakanda Forever" og mange andre visuelle effekter du kanskje har sett, ble laget av noen av våre tidligere studenter.
